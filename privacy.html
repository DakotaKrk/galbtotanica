Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Galbotanica Studio ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website.

Who We Are

Galbotanica Studio is a photography studio based in Sweden. For any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at: nataly.kyrk@gmail.com

Information We Collect

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This service may collect:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • Your approximate location (country/city level)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • How you arrived at our website

This data is anonymized and we cannot identify you personally from it.

Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display typography on our website. When you visit, your browser connects to Google's servers to load fonts, which means Google may receive your IP address.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential cookies: Remember your cookie consent preference
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics cookies to understand website usage (only with your consent)

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: Privacy Policy
  • Netlify (hosting): Privacy Policy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
